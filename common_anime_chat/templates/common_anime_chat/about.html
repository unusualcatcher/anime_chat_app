Aniconnect aims to connect users to other users who have watched the same anime as them.

How to use AniConnect?

Upon creating their accounts and logging in, users can head to their update profile page and link their MyAnimeList (MAL) accounts by entering their usernames in the input bar and clicking on "Link". Linking accounts may take a couple of seconds so please be patient.
In case the user does not have a MAL account or does not want to link it, they can manually enter the anime they have watched in the "Anime watched by you" input section. Pro tip: you can also link your MAL account and then add entries manually too, just be sure to click on the "Update Anime List" button afterward.
After you are done with this process, simply go to the home page and start chatting. Enjoy!
